## üöÄ Highlighted Projects

### 1. Space Telescope Shipping Container ‚Äî Capstone (Mech 4860)
- **What:** Designed and validated a shipping container for a high-value space telescope payload.  
- **How:** Systems engineering methodology, requirements flowdown, dynamic modeling, frequency-domain vibration analysis.  
- **Results:** Achieved payload protection, reduced vibration transmissibility, delivered system under \$10,000 budget.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Systems engineering, dynamic modeling, vibration isolation, V&V.

### 2. Aircraft Wing Design & Optimization (Mech 3520)
- **What:** Structural and aerodynamic optimization of aircraft wing.  
- **How:** CAD modeling, FEA, CFD simulations, iterative lift-to-drag ratio and weight optimization.  
- **Results:** Improved lift-to-drag, reduced weight, stress within allowable limits.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Aerodynamics, FEA, CFD, trade studies.

### 3. Aircraft Materials Selection & Manufacturing (Mech 4192)
- **What:** Optimal material selection for hollow circular beam under 4000 N flexural load.  
- **How:** Calculated modulus & tensile strength, applied Rule of Mixtures, evaluated fiber-matrix compatibility.  
- **Results:** Selected Carbon Fiber-Reinforced Epoxy, minimized weight to 0.122 kg.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Composite materials, lightweight design, manufacturing process selection.

### 4. Turboprop Propulsion System Design & Analysis (Mech 4200)
- **What:** Designed turboprop propulsion to meet shaft power & thrust efficiently.  
- **How:** Brayton-cycle modeling, propeller matching, parametric studies in MATLAB/Python.  
- **Results:** Achieved target power, high propeller efficiency, acceptable tip Mach numbers.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Gas turbine cycle, turboprop sizing, propulsion integration.

### 5. Miniature Turbojet Engine Performance Analysis (Mech 4200)
- **What:** Evaluated laboratory miniature turbojet performance.  
- **How:** Measured pressures, temperatures, fuel flow, RPM; applied thermodynamic calculations.  
- **Results:** Identified deviations from ideal cycle and component inefficiencies.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Propulsion testing, thermodynamics, data analysis.

### 6. Aircraft Landing Gear Vibration Analysis (Mech 3420)
- **What:** Dynamic analysis of landing gear system to identify natural frequencies & damping.  
- **How:** Impact hammer testing, accelerometer data, MATLAB frequency response and modal extraction.  
- **Results:** Dominant vibration modes identified; experimental validation of analytical models.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Structural dynamics, experimental modal analysis.

### 7. Classical Control Systems Design (Mech 3430)
- **What:** Feedback control design to meet stability and performance requirements.  
- **How:** Developed transfer functions, designed PI/PD/PID controllers, evaluated frequency-domain margins.  
- **Results:** Stable closed-loop response, improved accuracy, reduced steady-state error.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Control systems, root-locus, frequency-domain analysis.

### 8. Structural & Stress Analysis ‚Äî Beams, Trusses, Fuselage
- **What:** Optimized structural members, fuselage stringers, and hovercraft designs.  
- **How:** Classical mechanics, FEA, free-body diagrams, iterative design optimization, CFD for fluids.  
- **Results:** Improved material efficiency, stress within allowable limits, verified design requirements.  
- **Engineering Focus:** Mechanics of materials, thin-walled structures, CFD, lightweight design.
